Directions

Now the selection of stocks is completely open. Please use the stock sliders to select a portfolio with the maximum return with the minimum risk.

The "Bookmark portfolio" button will take a snapshot of your current investment selection. These are displayed on the risk/return plot with a black dot. You can click on the dots to restore your investment selections.

Each line in the risk/return plot shows how the current risk/return amount will change from changing the investment in each stock. The part below the line is how the risk/return will change if you reduce the amount invested. The part above the dot is how the risk/return will change if you increase the amount invested. There is a line for each stock and the line colors correspond to those in the legend shown next to the sliders.
